About Us

The Project:

TrackHub is an interactive music player that allows users to create an account, stream music, rate songs, chat with other members, and allows users to see a history of their songs. One of our goals when creating TrackHub was to make it cross platform, so we implemented it as a website available to run in any modern web browser. The main features that distinguish TrackHub from other music players are its rating/review, chat, and music video integration. .
